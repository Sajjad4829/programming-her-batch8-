Sajek Valley is a mesmerizing destination nestled in the Chittagong Hill Tracts of Bangladesh. It is renowned for its pristine natural beauty, panoramic landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. Located approximately 2000 feet above sea level, Sajek Valley offers a refreshing escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.Sajek Valley's remoteness and unspoiled beauty have made it a popular destination for eco-tourism. The local authorities and communities are working together to preserve the natural environment and promote sustainable tourism practices.
